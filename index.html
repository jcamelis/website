What brings together a diverse bunch of political scientists, coders, entrepreneurs, designers and social scientists? We all believe in innovating in the political system and we’ve decided to do something about it.

A question keeps us all awake at night: How can we open democracy’s bandwidth? We are convinced that internet has completely changed the way we communicate, the way we create, the way we share and collaborate, the way we produce culture, the way we transfer value... But Democracy is still the same!

ERROR 404: DEMOCRACY NOT FOUND

That’s the global answer to this clash between a system that doesn’t represent us anymore and citizens more and more used to representing themselves and structure Peer2Peer....

What can we do about it? We can create new instances of citizen activism and participation through technology. Our mission is to help design Democracy’s new Operating System.

Ambitious? well, yes. So what? We aim for the stars but are starting with one step: a software imagined for parliaments and other collective decision-making institutions that will allow citizens to get informed, join the conversation and vote how they want their representatives to vote.

Let’s hack the political system so it finally leaves the analogical age behind and joins the rest of us in the digital era.
